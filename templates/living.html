LIVING

On the first floor you enter the beautiful open loft style kitchen and dining room living space. The American style kitchen is constructed in solid oak with black neolith surface counter tops. Fitted with brand new integrated appliances: large range, oven, microwave, fridge / freezer, dishwasher and noiseless hidden ceiling extractor fan. The central island makes for a sociable cooking experience with friends and family, while the large windows and access to a balcony provides a ton of light into the living area. The space has been designed to include a large dining table and more relaxed seating area.

Adjacent to this living space is the glass and iron walled office space, with a feature brick wall and sliding doors, it has been designed to convert into an extra guest room as required.
