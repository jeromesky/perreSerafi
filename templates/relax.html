RELAX

Ascending to the top floor from the living area, you will find a laundry space, boiler area, and a toilet and sink, leading to a door for access to the back of the terrace and the outside water supply. An oak walkway to the left of the stairs leads to a large, light and airy living room with a 4 meter floor to ceiling window wall. This sliding structure opens the full length of the living area and allows for seamless access to the terrace to create an integrated indoor / outdoor living room providing both sunshine and shade.

The extraordinary large terrace is tiled in terracotta, with traditional blue tiled wall details. An irrigation system around the terrace enables large plants to survive in the heat, and the space allows for distinct seating, dining, barbeque and chill-out spaces.

The house has gas central heating, and like the air conditioning, this is regulated by zones for better comfort. Both systems are also remotely controllable via your phone once outside the property.

The parking space is conveniently located directly under the building, two floors down and accessed by lift or a staircase.
