YOUR HOME IN BARCELONA

This unusually large apartment in the heart of vibrant Gracia, one of the most sought-after neighbourhoods in the centre of Barcelona, has been completely renovated (and architect-designed) to effortlessly combine Gracia cool with all the necessities required for comfortable modern city living. It provides a rare retreat in this exceptional city, within minutes of all the action

With excellent shopping amenities all around (the 2 Mercado are less than 4 minutes walk), this social neighbourhood is full of character and has plenty of fantastic restaurants, bars and placas / terraces, Theartures. It is serviced by two underground stations and is minutes walk from the Diagonal, the famous Paseo de Gracia and is situated between three of Gaudi’s most famous landmarks.

Access to the House: The property has its own front door, gated and set back, with mailbox, direct to the street. On entrance, an airy marble hallway leads to a two level staircase which features movement activated lighting. There is a large basement storage room, followed by a welcoming entrance area with space for furniture, shoes and jackets. The central staircase linking the three floors features cast iron railings.
